[X_PADDING=12]BONUS SHOULDER SASH:
[X_PADDING=20]1. The Shoulder Sash System allows players of level 1 and above to wear a new piece
[X_PADDING=20]of equipment.
[X_PADDING=20]2. Shoulder Sash can absorb ALL weapon and armor bonuses except special weapon bonuses
[X_PADDING=20]and Mystical armor. The percentage of absorption depends on its degree of quality
[X_PADDING=20]of the Shoulder Sash , the higher the quality, the higher the percentage of absorption.
[X_PADDING=20]3. To enter the bonuses you have to go to the NPC Theowahdan on the map
[X_PADDING=20]Wonder Village.
[X_PADDING=20]4.You can purchase a 1st rank Shoulder Sash from NPC Theowahdan with the Thin Fabrics that
[X_PADDING=20]you will drop from metin.
[X_PADDING=20]5. You can remove bonuses from a Shoulder Sash using the remove stole bonus item.

[X_PADDING=12]MIGLIORAMENTO DI UNA STOLA:
[X_PADDING=20]1. To upgrade a Shoulder Sash, go to the NPC Theowahdan and click on Combination.
[X_PADDING=20]2. The upgrade ALWAYS destroys the second Shoulder Sash inserted, whether successful or not
[X_PADDING=20]in case of bankruptcy. In case there are bonuses in both Shoulder Sash , they do not
[X_PADDING=20]they will add up, but only those of the first Shoulder Sash will be considered, as this is what
[X_PADDING=20]is actually improved. The second has the sole function of "object for improvement".

[X_PADDING=20]Fine           5%
