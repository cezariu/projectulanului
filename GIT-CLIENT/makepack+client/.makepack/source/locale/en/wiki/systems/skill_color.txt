[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]STRUCTURE OF THE SYSTEM

[X_PADDING=12]1. When a skill reaches P by opening the skill panel above the skill icon at P it will appear a colored circle.
[X_PADDING=12]2. Clicking on the circle with the left mouse button will open the panel of the skill color system.
[X_PADDING=12]3. Each skill can be colored in 5 different colors at the same time, you can choose the various colors by clicking on the
[X_PADDING=12]numbers at the bottom of the panel.
[X_PADDING=12]4. Whenever you will  want to change the color of one of your abilities you will need to have a Rainbow Elixir
[X_PADDING=12]5. Using the arrow located on the right of the panel you can use the 3 available options, save color, insert color with
[X_PADDING=12]Hex code and use the predefinite color.
