[X_PADDING=12]1.The shamy support panel icon is located next to the inventory or you can open the panel
[X_PADDING=12]pressing the X button.

[X_PADDING=12]2.For unlocking the pannel of the shaman support you will need to have the item Unlock support Dragon Force or Unlock support Healing Force
[X_PADDING=12]Unlock support Healing Force 

[X_PADDING=12]3 If you have one of those items by clicking on it with the right button ,will appear a panel where you will ask
[X_PADDING=12]to insert a name and choose the sex of your support shaman.

[X_PADDING=12]will increase. The support shaman take xp only when is evocated and when monsters are killed.

[X_PADDING=12]5. The shaman will start with a basic equip,you will have the choice of choosing
[X_PADDING=12]the esthetic of him/she by opening the shop situated on the panel.

[X_PADDING=12]6. It's impossible to summon 2 shaman support together, and it's impossible to summon them on Guild Wars

[X_PADDING=12]7. You can change the name of your shaman by using the item  Magic of the name.

[X_PADDING=12]8. You can change the sex of your shaman by using the item Scroll of Transformation.
