[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]INFORMATION

[X_PADDING=12]1. The first 2 open clients will have the drop available, by opening the third client the drop will be blocked automatically
[X_PADDING=12]so it can only earn xp
[X_PADDING=12]2. It will be possible to activate and deactivate the drop to a character by clicking on the icon at the top left.
[X_PADDING=12]3. The drop can only be activated and deactivated in the Wonder Village map and you have to wait for a cooldown
[X_PADDING=12] of 5 seconds.
[X_PADDING=12]4. If you have more than 2 clients open and you enable the drop to the third client, you automatically disable the drop to the first client, and
[X_PADDING=12]so on in numerical order for other open clients.
