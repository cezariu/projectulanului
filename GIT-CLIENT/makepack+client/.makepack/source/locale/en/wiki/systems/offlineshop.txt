[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]STRUCTURE OF THE SYSTEM

[X_PADDING=12]1. You can open the offline shop panel by clicking on the bundle icon located on the minimap or by pressing
[X_PADDING=12]the Y key.
[X_PADDING=12]2. The offline shop consists of 9 sections:
[X_PADDING=20]I. My shop
[X_PADDING=20]II. List of shops
[X_PADDING=20]III. Warehouse
[X_PADDING=20]IV. My offers
[X_PADDING=20]V.  Search
[X_PADDING=20]VI. My searches
[X_PADDING=20]VII. Saved searches
[X_PADDING=20]VIII. My auctions
[X_PADDING=20]IX. Auctions in progress


[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=184; CENTER_ALIGN]

[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]FUNCTIONALITY OF THE VARIOUS SECTIONS

[X_PADDING=12]1. My shop: in this section you can create your offline shop, at the top right you will find a preview of the
[X_PADDING=12]shop you will use, by clicking on the arrows you can also choose the special shops but be careful,
[X_PADDING=12]to use the special shops you will need 1 Cashmere Bundle.

[X_PADDING=12]2. Store List: In this section you can look at all active offline stores.

[X_PADDING=12]3. Warehouse: in this section there are all the items that you have not sold and the revenue received from the sales.

[X_PADDING=12]4. Le mie offerte: in questa sezione sono elencate tutte le offerte che hai effetuato.

[X_PADDING=12]5. Search: in this section you can use the various filters to make a targeted search.

[X_PADDING=12]6. My searches: in this section you can see all the searches you have made.

[X_PADDING=12]7. Saved Searches: All saved search filters are listed in this section.

[X_PADDING=12]8. My auctions: in this section you can create an auction or check the status of one of your auctions in progress.

[X_PADDING=12]9. Auctions in progress: this section lists all the auctions still in progress.


[IMG=d:/ymir work/ui/wiki/divider_long.tga; Y=482; CENTER_ALIGN]
