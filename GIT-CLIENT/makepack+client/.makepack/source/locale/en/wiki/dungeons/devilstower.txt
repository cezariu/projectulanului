[CENTER_ALIGN;FONT_SIZE=18;COLOR=58ADFF]WHILE RUNNING THE DUNGEON

[X_PADDING=12;COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]FIRST FLOOR
[X_PADDING=20]1. Kill all monsters.

[X_PADDING=12;COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]SECOND FLOOR
[X_PADDING=20]1. Kill the Demon King.

[X_PADDING=12;COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]THIRD FLOOR
[X_PADDING=20]1. Destroy the Metin of Devil.
[X_PADDING=20]2. Destroy the Metin of Fall correct.

[X_PADDING=12;COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]FOURTH FLOOR
[X_PADDING=20]1. Find 5 Key Stone.
[X_PADDING=20]2. Drag the Key Stone to the Seals to move to the next floor.

[X_PADDING=12;COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]FIFTH FLOOR
[X_PADDING=20]1. Kill the Proud Demon King.
[X_PADDING=20]2. Use the Blacksmith to upgrade any item or click on it to proceed to the next floor.

[X_PADDING=12;COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]SIXTH FLOOR
[X_PADDING=20]1. Destroy all the Metins of Death
[X_PADDING=20]2. Destroy the Metin of Murder  to drop the  Unknown Old Chest.
[X_PADDING=20]3. You need to click on the Unknown Old Chest to find the Zin-San-Gui map.
[X_PADDING=20]4. Right-click on the Zin-Sa-Gui map to go to the next floor.

[X_PADDING=12;COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]SEVENTH FLOOR
[X_PADDING=20]1. Find the Zin-Bong-In Key.
[X_PADDING=20]2. Drag the Zin-Bong-In Key to Sa-Soe to move to the next floor.

[X_PADDING=12;COLOR=ef4f00;FONT_SIZE=15]EIGHTH FLOOR
[X_PADDING=20]1.  kill the Death Reaper.
